Long Provincial

Website Redesign Concept

Scroll down

Background

I was tasked with redesigning Long Provincial Vietnamese Restaurant's website based on a provided proto persona.

Role: User Experience Designer
Tools: Paper, Pencil, Sketch, Key Note, Adobe Photoshop
Sprint: 10 Days

Persona

Lauren Berlinger

The proto-persona provided is a 29 year old scriptwriter who cares about authenticity, exclusivity and uniqueness of purchases.

“It's not whether I can afford it, it's whether i can afford NOT to have it”

Lauren B.

Laura has a well developed collection of fashion and jewelry and is big fan of 'retro-chic.’ When something catches her eye she must have it, whatever the cost. She considers herself a digital native and a smart consumer, and she loves to download new apps, check out technology trends, and show off her purchases. As a writer, she has a keen eye for sharp copy and likes brands who express their personality through their words.

Tech empathy: High | Purchase cycle: Short | Has: MacBook Air, iPhone, iPad

What’s the nature of the project?

My research goal is to discover what improvements can be made to Long Provincial’s website and improve the overall user experience for the persona. I want to learn pain points and user needs/wants when visiting the website. I also want to learn the habits and personality of the users so I have a better understanding of their thought process and utilize that information to redesign Long Provicincial’s website into something more useful.

Who are the users and the business?

The primary users tend to work near Long Provincial restaurant in downtown Seattle. Long Provincial heaviest foot traffic is during lunch time. They are open late, however due to parking and location, most of their patrons are people who work nearby, coming down for lunch.

What are the design options and how decisions are made?

The design option I chose to move forward with contains many photos based on the persona’s needs. Other elements included adding more mobile app integration because it is a favored channel by my persona. Because she prefers a quick and efficient check out, I decided to move forward with a 3rd party ordering with eat24. I chose to move forward with eat24 because that would be the most efficient way for the user to check out, she would be able to order from many different restaurants and will not have to worry about logging into another account and ordering directly through the website.

What are the constraints and limitations?

The primary limitation is the resources for conducting research and the manpower to conduct thorough research.

How to validate your design?

I validated my design by creating tasks for users, and interviewing them afterwards, asking if the design was visually appealing and if they felt confident if they knew what they were ordering. The feature to order online was also introduced with this iteration which allowed users to use eat24.com to order food.

What will be your next step?

The next steps will be creating an iOS version to fully address my persona’s pain points that will allow ordering from mobile as well as plenty of images.
